Salumarada Thimmakka

Saalumarada Thimmakka is an environmentslist from India and she is referred to as "Mother of Trees". She is noted for her work in planting and nurturing 385 banyan trees along a 4 kilometer stretch. The reason for her fame is that she has planted 8000 trees in 80 years.
